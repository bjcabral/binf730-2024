FASTA Input

FASTA stands for Fast All, derived from previous formats such as FASTA -P for protein sequences and FAST-N for nucleotides. (All) indicates it works with any alphabet, protein of nucleotide FASTA is a widely used text-based format for representing nucleotides or amino acid. The format is very simple: A header line starting with greater-than symbol (>) followed by the sequence identifier an optional description, and the sequence data in the next line Example: >Alien| Alien dna found on the moon ATTTCCCGGCCTTG
