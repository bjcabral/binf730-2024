Phylogenetic Tree Construction

To infer the evolutionary relationships or construct phylogenetic tress, common methods are used including UPGMA (Unweighted Pair Group Method with Arithmetic Mean): This method assumes a constant rate of evolution, and builds the tree b iteratively clustering the closest sequences. Neighbor-Joining (NJ): Does not assume constant rate of evolution and construct the tree by minimizing the total branch length. WPGMA (Weighted Pair Group Method with Arithmetic Mean): Similar to UPGMA, but different on how distances are calculated. This application implements UPGMA and Neighbor-Joining
